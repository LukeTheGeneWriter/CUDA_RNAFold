    .. container:: detailed-description

        This array contains the density of states for an RNA sequences after a call to :ref:`subopt_par() <doxid-group__subopt__wuchty_1gaa1e1e7031a948ebcb39a9d58d1e9842c>` , :ref:`subopt() <doxid-group__subopt__wuchty_1ga700f662506a233e42dd7fda74fafd40e>` or :ref:`subopt_circ() <doxid-group__subopt__wuchty_1ga8634516e4740e0b6c9a46d2bae940340>` .
        
        
        
        .. admonition:: Pre-Condition
        
            Call one of the functions :ref:`subopt_par() <doxid-group__subopt__wuchty_1gaa1e1e7031a948ebcb39a9d58d1e9842c>` , :ref:`subopt() <doxid-group__subopt__wuchty_1ga700f662506a233e42dd7fda74fafd40e>` or :ref:`subopt_circ() <doxid-group__subopt__wuchty_1ga8634516e4740e0b6c9a46d2bae940340>` prior accessing the contents of this array
